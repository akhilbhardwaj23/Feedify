[Problem Statement]

Design a WebApp displaying interactive RSS feed information of multiple sources.

[Minimum Requirement]

— Visually interactive design to help read RSS feeds.

— Capability to dynamically add and remove multiple sources.

— Consider UI/UX factors such as typography, subtle patterns/textures, user view point, icon library.

[Plus Point]

— Integrating plugins, external libraries (plus if it is your own creation).

— You may add portfolio page comprising awesome work you have done on UI/UX. You may also link your dribbble, behance, deviantART or any external source directing to your work.

— Use of extensive HTML5 / CSS3 feature, your favorite client side scripting framework (preferably ExtJS) and any design pattern library.

— Use your imagination to come up with additional UI/UX features.
